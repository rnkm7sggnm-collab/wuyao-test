# Video Rules

## Principles

- Prioritize hook, rhythm, visual clarity, and production value.
- Keep concepts shootable or generatable.
- Match the format: short video, ad, trailer, tutorial, vlog, or cinematic scene.
- Avoid bloated explanations; make each shot or line useful.
- Keep the user's tone and intended platform in mind.

## Working Format

- Concept: one clear sentence.
- Structure: hook, development, payoff.
- Shot list: simple visual actions.
- Notes: style, pacing, music, and key assets.
